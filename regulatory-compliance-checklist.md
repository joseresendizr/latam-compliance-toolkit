# Risk Assessment Matrix
## Third-Party & Client Onboarding Risk Scoring

Use this matrix to assign a risk score during onboarding. Total score determines the level of due diligence required.

---

## Scoring guide

For each category, select the score that best matches the customer or third party being assessed. Sum all scores to determine the overall risk level.

## Risk scoring summary

**Total score: ___ / 25**

| Total score | Risk level | Due diligence required |
|---|---|---|
| 5 – 9 | Low | Standard KYC — basic ID and address verification |
| 10 – 15 | Medium | Standard KYC + source of funds declaration + annual review |
| 16 – 20 | High | Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) + senior approval + quarterly review |
| 21 – 25 | Critical | EDD + board/compliance committee approval + monthly review + SAR consideration |

---

## Due diligence actions by risk level

### Low risk
- [ ] Official ID verified
- [ ] Address verified
- [ ] Sanctions screening completed
- [ ] Annual review scheduled

### Medium risk
- [ ] All Low risk actions
- [ ] Source of funds declaration obtained
- [ ] Business activity verified
- [ ] PEP screening completed
- [ ] Semi-annual review scheduled

### High risk
- [ ] All Medium risk actions
- [ ] Enhanced background check
- [ ] Senior management approval documented
- [ ] Ongoing transaction monitoring activated
- [ ] Quarterly review scheduled

### Critical risk
- [ ] All High risk actions
- [ ] Compliance committee or board approval
- [ ] Legal counsel review
- [ ] Monthly transaction review
- [ ] SAR filing assessed
- [ ] Relationship may be declined
